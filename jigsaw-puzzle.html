About The Game

Welcome to "I'm not a Monster," a delightful entry into the cozyverse that proves monsters can be friends, too! This isn't your typical spooky creature feature; it's a heartwarming and beautifully illustrated jigsaw puzzle game designed to help you relax and de-stress. The game invites you into a soft, gentle world where the only goal is to piece together charming images of adorable, friendly monsters. Each puzzle features a unique character with big eyes and a friendly smile, set against a calm and soothing backdrop. The art style is minimalist yet full of personality, creating an atmosphere of pure comfort.

How to Play

The gameplay is simple, intuitive, and perfect for anyone looking for a peaceful escape. Your main objective is to assemble the jigsaw puzzles. You'll be presented with a scattered collection of puzzle pieces and a faint outline of the final image. Your task is to drag and drop each piece into its correct position on the canvas. When a piece is placed correctly, it will lock into place with a satisfying, gentle click.

Getting started is effortless. Use your mouse to click and hold a puzzle piece, drag it across the screen to where you think it fits, and release the mouse button to drop it. The controls are smooth and responsive, ensuring a frustration-free session of fun. If you're ever stuck, you can toggle a preview of the final image to guide you.

Find Your Zen

There are no timers, no scores to beat, and no pressure. You can take as much time as you need to contemplate the shapes and colors, making it a truly meditative experience. This game is a perfect example of how the cozyverse can turn a simple activity into a moment of pure zen. It's an ideal choice for a short break during a busy day or a longer session of unwinding in the evening.
