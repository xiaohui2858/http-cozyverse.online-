The premise is beautifully simple, yet the game offers layers of engaging complexity. You are presented with several test tubes, each containing a mix of different colored liquids. Your goal is to sort these liquids so that each tube contains only a single color. This task requires foresight, planning, and a touch of strategy, making it an excellent free brain-teaser. As you progress, the levels introduce more tubes and colors, offering a steady, rewarding difficulty curve that keeps you engaged without ever feeling overwhelming. If you're looking to play a free puzzle game that is both stimulating and soothing, you've found the perfect match.

How to Play

  • Objective: Sort the colored water in the tubes until all colors are in separate tubes.
  • Controls: Simply click (or tap on mobile) on a tube to select it, then click on another tube to pour the top layer of liquid into it.
  • Rule: You can only pour water into another tube if it is empty or if the top layer of water is the same color.

Tips & Tricks for Success

  1. Utilize Empty Tubes: An empty tube is your most powerful tool. Use it as a temporary holding space to shuffle liquids around.
  2. Plan Ahead: Before making a move, try to visualize the next 2-3 steps. Avoid trapping colors you'll need later.
  3. Don't Be Afraid to Restart: If you feel stuck, the 'Restart' button is your friend. It’s a no-pressure cozy game, after all!
